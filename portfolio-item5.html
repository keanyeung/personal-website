ME100 Term Design Project

sept 2021 - dec 2021

This project was part of the "Introduction to Mechanical Engineering Practice 1" or "ME100" course. For our term design project, our task was to design and manufacture a toy that was to be submitted to a large toy company.

Our group decided to make a "Soccer Penalty Shootout" game, in which there are two players: The shooter and the Goalie. The shooter uses the shooting machine to aim the ball towards the goal in the desired position. Spin, power and height can be adjusted by the player. The goalie's objective is to prevent the ball from scoring into the net. The goalie can be moved left and right by the second player.
