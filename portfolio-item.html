UW Orbital Chassis Design

sept 2021 - present

From the beginning of my 1A term, I have been an active member of the Mechanical Team as a Part of UW Orbital Design Team. Our goal is to design and manufacture a CubeSat in hopes of deploying it to space as part of the Canadian Satelite Design Challenge.

As part of the team, I have been helping to develop different parts of the chassis for our CubeSat. Some of my contributions include: development of the side panel, end panel and corner rail, ideation and design of a mechanism for interaction between the electronics of the CubeSat and the deployment switches.

I have also been able to learn and use different maechanical stimunations as a part of the team. I partaked in SOLIDWORKS FEA training to optimize the structure and design of our chassis. NX Thermal Simulations was another feature that I have been fortunate to learn about. This analysis allows for the team to account for material propoerty and structrue changes while in orbit around the earth.
